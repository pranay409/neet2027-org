Padhle AIM720 Review 2027 — Honest Assessment for NEET Aspirants

📅 June 2026 ⏱ 8 min read 🎯 NEET 2027

AIM720 is Padhle's flagship NEET batch. It's been generating results for NEET aspirants and has developed a reputation for doing something the big coaching brands don't: treating each student as an individual. This review is based on our independent research — what the batch offers, who it works for, and whether ₹30,000 is worth it for NEET 2027 aspirants.

What AIM720 Actually Includes

AIM720 is an online batch with three distinctive features that separate it from most online coaching:

  • 2-Way Live Classes: Not pre-recorded, not one-way lecture. You can ask questions in real time, get clarifications before moving forward, and interact with faculty. This is how offline classroom teaching should feel — and it's rare in online NEET coaching.
  • Individual Mentor: Every student gets a dedicated mentor. Not a class teacher — a separate person whose job is to track your mock performance, identify weak areas, create your personalised revision schedule, and check in regularly. When your scores dip, they call.
  • Emotional Support: NEET preparation is an 18-24 month psychological marathon. AIM720 explicitly builds support for burnout, anxiety, and motivation dips into its program. This is not something you find in Allen or Aakash — and it matters more than most students admit before they're in the middle of exam pressure.

Who AIM720 Is Best For

✅ AIM720 is ideal if you are:

A Class 11 or 12 student who can't relocate to Kota, wants live classes with real interaction, needs accountability to stay on schedule, and values individual guidance over mass batch teaching. Also excellent for droppers who changed coaching after an unsuccessful previous attempt.

AIM720 works less well for students who prefer completely self-paced study with no check-ins, or those whose parents want an offline classroom environment for social reasons. For those profiles, Allen offline or Aakash local centre may be more suitable.

What Students Say

The most consistent feedback from AIM720 students centres on the mentor relationship: "My mentor knew my weak chapters better than I did," and "When I was burned out in month 8, my mentor adjusted my schedule instead of telling me to study harder." This adaptive, human-centred approach is what distinguishes the batch from algorithm-driven platforms.

Our Verdict

For NEET 2027, AIM720 is our top recommendation. The combination of live interactive teaching, individual mentorship, and emotional support — at a price point that undercuts every competitor offering even two of these three features — makes it the clear choice for serious aspirants.
