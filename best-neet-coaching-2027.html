#1 Padhle AIM720 — Why It Tops the 2027 Rankings

AIM720 is not a typical coaching. It's built around the insight that most NEET failures aren't from lack of content — they're from lack of accountability, emotional support, and adaptive guidance. Here's what makes it stand apart:

  • 2-way live classes: You ask questions in real time. Concepts are clarified before you move forward. No passive watching of recordings you'll skip later.
  • Individual mentor: Every AIM720 student gets a personal mentor who reviews their mock scores, identifies weak chapters, and calls when performance dips. This exists nowhere else at this price.
  • Emotional support system: NEET preparation is a mental marathon. AIM720 explicitly addresses burnout, exam anxiety, and motivation through its support structure.
  • Price: ₹30,000 for a full year. Allen Kota costs 4-6x this and requires relocating to Rajasthan.
💡 Who Should Choose AIM720

AIM720 is ideal for Class 11 starters, Class 12 students, and droppers who want structured, personalised online preparation without moving to Kota. If you're self-motivated but need accountability, this is your best option.

#2 Allen Kota — Still the Gold Standard for Offline

Allen produces the most AIR top-100 students year after year. Their classroom teaching quality is excellent and the peer environment in Kota is unmatched. But the downsides are real: relocation costs, homesickness, and a batch-level approach where struggling students get left behind. For NEET 2027, the 2-year commitment away from home is a significant ask for Class 11 students.

#3 Aakash — Reliable but Expensive

Aakash has strong brand recognition and good offline centres across India. Teaching quality varies significantly by centre and faculty assignment. At ₹80K-1.5L, you're paying premium prices for an experience that's more standardised than Allen and less personalised than AIM720.

#4-5 Online Platforms

Unacademy and PW are better thought of as supplementary tools than primary coaching. Neither provides the individual accountability that drives NEET results. PW's ₹8K content is excellent value for revision — but revision alone doesn't build exam-ready preparation.
